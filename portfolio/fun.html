Fun Activities



Bicycling is a big activity for both of us. This picture reminds me of that feelig of being on a bike and just having fun exploring.



Cross-Country skiing is also a lot of fun. Especially when the snow is perfect and the scenery is a beautiful forest in the north country.



I like to play certain board games and certain card games. I liked the card game Canasta, but not many people play that anymore. My other favorite is Bridge. In any event it is always best when dealt Aces.



Lately I've tried to paint some pictures. Mostly I've mimiced other art. It is a lot cheaper to make it yourself than buy an original and it adds some interesting ambiance. At left is my attempt to draw Van Gogh's "Sun Flowers". How do you like it?

Travel



Our first trip was to Glacier Park. Since then we've done quite a few more and they have all been special, just like that first one.



We have been to Mexico quite a few times. The beach at Tulum in Quinta Roo, Mexico is as nice of ocean beach as one can imagine. Going to Mexico around the end of winter is very invigorating.



I think there are other wonderful places in the world besides Minnesota and Mexico and one of them is Italy. We have been lucky to have visited Europe a few times and most lucky to have included Italy twice. The image at left of Cinque Terre is hardly sufficient to convey how nice it really is.
